Our Story

Rooted in
tradition.

The Olive Table was born from a simple idea: bring the warmth of Mediterranean home cooking to the heart of Long Beach. We source our ingredients from local farmers and trusted suppliers who share our belief that quality begins at the source.

Every recipe on our menu has a story — passed down through families, refined over years, and served with the care of a home-cooked meal.
